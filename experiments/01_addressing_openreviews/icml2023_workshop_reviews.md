## Issues Implied by Later Reviews

Even though this submission was accepted, the following issues were raised at ICLR 2024 and ICLR 2025 and may trace back to the workshop version:

1. Narrow dataset selection (PubMed, USPTO only)
2. Task2Vec methodology not sufficiently explained
3. Practical utility of the metric not demonstrated
4. Missing comparison to simpler diversity metrics
5. Claims about "paradigm shift" overstated given existing diversity literature
