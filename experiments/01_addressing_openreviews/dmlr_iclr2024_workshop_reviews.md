---

## Review Status

This workshop accepted the paper as a poster. No public per-reviewer detailed notes are visible via the OpenReview API — only the acceptance decision is recorded.

---

## Notes

- Second workshop acceptance for the work.
- DMLR is specifically focused on data-centric ML, suggesting the community found the data diversity framing relevant.
- Full-conference rejections (ICLR 2024 full, ICLR 2025) indicate that workshop-level acceptance does not resolve the deeper empirical and novelty concerns.

---

## Issues Implied by Surrounding Full-Conference Reviews

Despite workshop acceptance, the following issues from ICLR 2024/2025 reviews apply here:

1. Empirical evidence limited to 2-3 unrepresentative datasets
2. No comparison to Vendi Score or embedding-based baselines
3. Cross-entropy as the sole evaluation metric (no downstream task benchmarks)
4. Inconsistent correlation between diversity and performance
5. Task2Vec motivation and description insufficient for main paper
6. Missing citations to recent data-centric work (D4, FineWeb, DataComp-LM, etc.)
